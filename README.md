## 🌍 Our Story

### About Unico-Apply
We are a team of passionate South Africans dedicated to making university applications accessible, simple, and stress-free for every learner in our country.

Unico-Apply was born from a simple observation: too many talented learners miss out on higher education opportunities not because they are not qualified, but because the application process is confusing, time-consuming, and overwhelming — especially for first-generation university applicants.

We exist to change that.

---

## 🎯 Our Mission

### Unlocking Higher Education for Every South African Learner

Our mission is to remove the barriers that prevent learners from accessing higher education.

We believe that every learner deserves a fair chance at success. That’s why we simplify the entire application journey by:

- Handling university applications on behalf of learners through our expert Unico Admins
- Ensuring accuracy, completeness, and on-time submissions
- Providing centralized access to all key resources
  - University information
  - APS calculators
  - Document management tools
  - Application tracking

We don’t just help with applications — we guide learners toward their future.
